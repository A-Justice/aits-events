A Pan African Industrial trade promotion platform

The Africa Industrial Trade Show is a pan African Industrial trade promotion platform to highlight Africas biggest and foremost industrial parks to investors and entrepreneurs. Powered by the Economic Zones Chamber, the event promotes Africas Investment opportunities through Special Economic Zones.
AITS provides a unique platform for local and multinational companies in agro-processing, pharmaceuticals, garments and textiles, materials and metal fabrications among others to interact and network to build lasting collaborations as industry stakeholders in the economic zones universe for an environment that stimulates economic activity, investment attraction and manufacturing competitiveness.

Modality and Event Flow

The Africa Industrial Trade Show hosts high level Policy forum followed by thematic panel discussions and in-focus outlook sessions combining government representatives and captains of industry operating in Industrial Zones.
The event also sponsors leading African Industrial Park visits public and private, Investor matchmaking, B2B and C sessions for FDI attraction, Exhibition Booths for top manufacturing clusters spanning Pharma, Textile, Automotive, Agro, Logistics, Light and Heavy Manufacturing.
AITS 2026 will serve as an attraction point for pan-African trade promotion across Micro-Small-Medium Enterprises ad Large Corporates, Academia and Innovation hubs, Development Finance Institutions and Private Equity Investors with over 1,000 expected patrons in attendance.
